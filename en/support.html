Bunniki Support

Official support page for Bunniki questions, bug reports, and App Store support requests for macOS and iPadOS.

Contact

Email: shteosis@gmail.com

Helpful Information to Include

  • Your device type, operating system version, and Bunniki app version
  • Which Provider you were using, if any
  • The action that failed: wiki generation, query, source linking, Provider setup, and so on
  • What you expected to happen and what actually happened
  • Whether the issue occurred on Mac, iPad, simulator, or TestFlight/App Store build
  • Error messages or screenshots with private or sensitive source text removed

Support Scope

  • Installation and launch issues
  • Source attachment and output library setup
  • Wiki generation, wiki refresh, query, and web wiki view issues
  • OpenAI API, Codex OAuth, Local API, and Apple Foundation Models Provider connection issues
  • iPadOS advertising display or sponsorship banner questions
  • App Store review follow-up and policy questions

Response Note

Support is provided on a best-effort basis. Please avoid sending sensitive personal data or source documents unless they are strictly needed to reproduce a problem.
